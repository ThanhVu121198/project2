All Kinds of Creation With Bamboo

Bamboo is the perfect choice if you want to add wood tones to your interior because they soften the atmosphere in your home and make it more comfortable and inviting. They come in a variety of sizes and can make hundreds of eye-catching crafts, check out the ways below and try making your own bamboo ornament!
What do you think about this table when using bamboo to make table legs? Use different sizes of bamboo to create a fun look and it will attract the attention of your visitors.

1. Make bamboo a part of your dining table decor by making these adorable napkin rings.

2. Do you want to give your old vase a new coat using bamboo sticks? Even better when you can paint your favorite color on it.

3. Bamboo sticks can become a part of your kitchen because of their ability to hold knives!

4. Cut the bamboo into small pieces and use them as pots to plant herbs like herbs for your garden.
