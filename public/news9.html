Eye-catching Hexagonal Home Decorations

The hexagon, aka honeycomb shape has really gained popularity when it comes to interior design. If you are a fan of geometric patterns then scroll down and check out these eye-catching hexagonal home decor items that are sure to amaze you. All of the crafts below are suitable for most home styles and interiors, so what are you waiting for? Pick the one you like best and make it yourself. Hexagonal objects seem to have an enchanting charm that no one can resist. Discover it for yourself!

1. Stylish watches

What do you think if this hexagonal clock is hung in your living room? Its shape is really elegant and delicate. With just two pieces of pine put together, choose your favorite color to paint one of them, then drill a hole in the middle and attach the watch kit and you're done! Very simple but beautiful, isn't it?

2. Unique photo frames

If your wall has only one smooth paint color, you should quickly start creating a work of art to remove this dullness. These hexagonal picture frames will be more fancy than the traditional rectangular or circular picture frames and you can make them yourself in no time. You choose a photo frame from soft wood, or easier, use cardboard and collage to create a truly unique design of your own.

3. Shelves

It is not necessary that the entire shelf is hexagonal. Inside this honeycomb shelf are other shapes such as trapezoids, triangles, etc., creating a rather unique display.

4. Dinner table

A rather fancy hexagonal dining table you are sure to love. It just brings people closer together but the space we have is just enough. With the wood cutting technique, you can completely create this table yourself.

5. Notebook

The notebook you usually use to take notes should also reflect a bit of your personality, so if you are a creative person, you should try to decorate the notebook with a hexagon like above as soon as possible. They will bring joy to you every time you open the notebook.
